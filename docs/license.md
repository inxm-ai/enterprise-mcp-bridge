## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I use this in my company?

Yes! You can use, modify, and deploy Enterprise MCP Bridge in your company without restrictions.

### Do I need to open source my modifications?

Only if you **distribute** the software. If you use it internally without distributing to external parties, you don't need to share your modifications.

### Can I offer this as a hosted service?

Yes! Running it as a SaaS/hosted service doesn't require you to open source your modifications (though we encourage it).

### Can I create a proprietary plugin/extension?
